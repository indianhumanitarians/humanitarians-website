How it works

A clear path from need to earning.

Verification, practical support, follow-up, and public reporting — every step designed to protect dignity and create real outcomes.

01

Give with clarity

Donors see public, aggregated reports while private recipient and donor details stay fully protected. Zakat and Sadaqah are tracked in separate ledgers.

02

Support earning

Funds go toward practical turning points — a sewing machine, a course, a tool kit, shop stock, or mobility support. Not one-time relief where income is possible.

03

Mentor the next step

Volunteers help students and job seekers with resumes, interviews, communication, coding, and career direction. Guidance that changes trajectories.

04

Refer responsibly

Cases are reviewed before any support is arranged. Community members refer cases they personally vouch for. No anonymous submissions.

Our model

Built for lasting impact, not just relief.

Livelihood, skills, and mentorship for people to stand on their own feet — not just receive one-time aid.

✅

Verified cases

Every recipient is assessed by the team before any commitment. Zakat and Sadaqah are tracked in separate, public ledgers.

🔧

Tools & assets

We fund the missing step: capital, tools, machines, stock, courses, or job-readiness — whatever enables income.

🎓

IIT mentorship

Mentors from IITs, colleges, and professional backgrounds guide students toward stable, dignified employment.

📊

Public reporting

Monthly aggregated reports published openly — no recipient names, IDs, or payment details ever disclosed.

Transparency

You deserve to know where it goes.

Every rupee tracked. Every case anonymized. Every report published.

📋

Verified cases only

Each case is individually assessed before any funds are committed. No anonymous referrals, no unchecked disbursements.

⚖️

Zakat & Sadaqah separated

Funds are never mixed. Zakat is tracked and disbursed according to Islamic principles. Each has its own public ledger.

🔒

Privacy first

No recipient names, phone numbers, Aadhaar, donor details, or payment IDs are ever published. Dignity is non-negotiable.
